MEDICINAL PLANTS

Types of Medicinal Plants


Aloe Vera

  • Known for its soothing, skin healing properties
  • Used in burn treatment and cosmetic products
  • Contains antioxidants and antibacterial substances
  • Supports digestive health and relieves constipation


Mango

  • Rich in vitamins A, C, and E, plus beta-carotene
  • Contains compounds with antioxidant properties
  • May aid in maintaining overall heart health
  • Helps in digestion due to its fiber content


Mint

  • Used to relieve indigestion and reduce stomach aches
  • Has a cooling effect that can soothe sore muscles when applied topically
  • Improves oral health and reduces bad breath
  • May improve brain function and reduce symptoms of depression


Neem

  • Has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory effects
  • Traditionally used to treat skin conditions like eczema and acne
  • Supports dental health in products like toothpaste
  • Used as a natural pesticide in organic farming


Peepal

  • Leaves are known for their wound-healing properties
  • Considered useful in treating asthma and diabetes
  • Has astringent properties, beneficial for skin health
  • Bark and leaves are used in various Ayurvedic medicines


Tulsi

  • Adaptogen that helps the body manage stress
  • Boosts the immune system with its antiviral properties
  • May help in reducing blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Often used in teas and supplements for its therapeutic effects
